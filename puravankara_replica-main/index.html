About Puravankara Lokhandwala

About Puravankara Lokhandwala

Puravankara Limited introduces an iconic redevelopment at Lokhandwala Circle, Andheri West, redefining upscale living in one of Mumbai’s most prestigious neighbourhoods. Spread across 3 acres, this premium address features elegantly designed 2, 3 and 4 BHK Vaastu-compliant residences equipped with smart home automation and contemporary architectural detailing. Rising across 6 towers with 27 habitable floors, the project offers 2-level basement and 4-level podium parking, along with an elevated eco-deck on the 5th floor and more than 50 carefully curated lifestyle amenities.

Strategically positioned with excellent access to Oshiwara Metro Station, New Link Road and Andheri Station, residents enjoy seamless connectivity to major commercial and social landmarks. Surrounded by renowned malls, hospitals and educational institutions, this redevelopment promises a refined gated community experience at the heart of Andheri West.

Discover premium living with the puravankara new launch located at puravankara lokhandwala circle, one of the most desirable residential hubs in Mumbai. This landmark development by puravankara offers thoughtfully designed residences that combine modern architecture with everyday comfort.

About Puravankara Limited

With a trusted legacy since 1975, Puravankara stands among India’s most respected real estate developers, shaping skylines across major cities including Bangalore, Mumbai, Chennai, and Pune. Delivering over 86 completed projects across 9 cities and earning more than 200 awards, the brand is known for quality that lasts and customer trust built over decades. Puravankara leads with a strong commitment to sustainable luxury, integrating eco-conscious design, energy-efficient construction, advanced water and waste management systems, and expansive green landscapes. Focused on innovation and environmental responsibility, Puravankara continues to create future-ready homes that enrich modern living while respecting nature.
